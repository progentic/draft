## What Is DRAFT?

DRAFT is an open source desktop application for researchers, students, professionals, and anyone writing documents that depend on sources, structure, and careful review.

It brings research, analysis, formatting, and text review into one focused workspace so you can spend less time managing tools and more time improving the work.

## Capability Overview

### Document Research

DRAFT helps you keep sources, citations, bibliographies, and reference details close to the writing itself. It is designed for work where every claim needs a clear connection to evidence.

### Analysis

DRAFT helps you examine the strength, clarity, and support behind your document. It is built to make arguments easier to inspect, compare, and refine before the final draft leaves your desk.

### Formatting

DRAFT helps keep document structure, headings, citations, and output requirements consistent. It reduces the late-stage cleanup that often happens after the writing is already hard enough.

### Text-analysis

DRAFT helps review grammar, tone, clarity, cohesion, and flow. It supports better writing without taking control away from the author.

## Why DRAFT?

Research-heavy writing is difficult because the work is spread across too many places. Notes live in one tool. Sources live in another. Formatting happens somewhere else. Review often comes last.

DRAFT is built to reduce that friction.

It keeps the document, the evidence, and the review process closer together. The result is a writing workflow that is easier to understand, easier to check, and easier to trust.

## How It Helps

DRAFT is designed to support the full path from early research to polished document:

- Organize sources around the work you are writing.
- Keep citations and references easier to review.
- Check whether the document is clear, consistent, and well supported.
- Improve wording, tone, structure, and flow.
- Prepare document-ready output with fewer manual cleanup steps.
- Keep work local-first and privacy-conscious.
